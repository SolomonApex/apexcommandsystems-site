What the Transformation Is

Not an experiment. A permanent rebuild.

The Apex AI Operations Sprint™ is built for service businesses with 5 to 150 people and one or two clear operational pain points. The Transformation is built for something different.

Organizations with multiple departments. Complex operations that don't map neatly to a 30-day implementation. Industry-specific requirements. Leadership teams that need a partner who can think at the level of the whole organization, not just one workflow.

This is a full audit of how the organization runs, followed by a custom AI implementation roadmap, done-for-you system builds across every applicable area, full team training at scale, and an ongoing support structure designed to keep the operation growing. Scope

What a Transformation engagement includes.

Every Transformation is scoped custom. The components below are consistent across every engagement — the depth and timeline vary based on the organization.

Phase 1

Full Operational Audit

Every department. Every workflow. Every tool. We map how the organization actually operates — not how leadership thinks it operates. Bottlenecks, redundancies, and AI opportunities identified and ranked.

Phase 2

Custom AI Implementation Roadmap

A complete system design: every AI opportunity prioritized, scoped, and sequenced. Presented to leadership before a single system is built. You approve the roadmap before we move to implementation.

Phase 3

Done-For-You Build

Full implementation across all approved systems. Built inside your existing tools. Configured to your operations. Nothing delivered as a prototype — every system ships production-ready.

Phase 4

Team Training

Training delivered to every relevant team member. Department-specific sessions where needed. Full documentation library. Your team owns what we built — and knows how to use it.

Ongoing

Support Structure

An ongoing support structure designed for the scale of your organization. Not a retainer bolt-on — a purpose-built post-launch structure included in the engagement scope.

When Needed

Onsite Work

Jeff travels onsite when the engagement requires it. Some organizations need a physical presence during the audit or training phases. That is available and scoped into the proposal when applicable.

Right Fit

Who the Transformation is for.

  • Businesses with complex operations spanning multiple departments or service lines
  • Organizations where a 30-day Sprint does not cover the scope of what needs to change
  • Established businesses with real revenue — not experiments or early-stage ventures
  • C-suite leaders and executive teams who want a strategic operational partner, not a vendor
  • Industries with specific compliance, workflow, or integration requirements beyond standard implementation
  • Leaders who have tried other approaches and need someone to do the work, not advise on it

Not sure which is right?

If you're a service business with fewer than 150 people and a specific operational pain point, the Sprint is likely the right starting point. The Transformation is for organizations where the scope is larger than what a 30-day engagement can address.
