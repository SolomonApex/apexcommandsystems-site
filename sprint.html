The Process

How the 30 days work.

STEP 01

Diagnostic

We map your entire operation: how leads come in, how clients are managed, where time is lost, and where AI can create the most immediate impact. Nothing gets built until we understand exactly what we're building and why.

STEP 02

Design

We design the two systems specific to your business. The architecture, the logic, the voice, the triggers — all scoped to your actual tools and existing workflows before a single system is built.

STEP 03

Build

We build and install. Inside your CRM, your communication platform, your existing stack. You review at each stage. Nothing ships without your sign-off.

STEP 04

Train + Hand Off

Your team is trained on every system we built. Full documentation, recorded walkthrough, and a 30-day support window. You own everything. We leave nothing dependent on us being around.
