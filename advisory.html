What the Advisory Is

Operational continuity, not hand-holding.

Most implementation engagements end at delivery. The systems go live, the consultant leaves, and six months later half the automations are broken or ignored. Your team reverted to what they knew. The investment stalled.

The Advisory prevents that. Each month, we review what's running, identify what's drifted, build what's next, and make sure the whole operation is moving forward — not just staying put.

This is not a check-in service. It is active operations management from someone who already knows your business, your tools, and your team.

"The businesses that win with AI are the ones that keep building after the first systems go live. The Advisory is how that compounding happens."

Monthly Scope

What happens every month.

Every Advisory month includes the same four components. No surprises, no scope creep, no need to ask what you're paying for.

Monthly

Performance Report

A clear summary of how your systems are performing: response rates, engagement metrics, time saved, bottlenecks identified. You always know what the systems are doing and where the gaps are.

Monthly

One New System or Campaign

Every month, we build one new automation, sequence, or AI-powered process. The operation keeps expanding. Month 6 looks nothing like month 1 — and that is the point.

Monthly

Strategy Call

A focused 30-minute call to review the month, align on priorities, and plan the next build. Not a status update. A working session with someone who understands your operation at depth.

Ongoing

Optimization + Support

Existing systems are monitored and adjusted as your operations change. When something breaks, drifts, or can be improved — we catch it and fix it. You do not manage this.
